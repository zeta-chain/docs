### FUNGIBLE_MODULE_ADDRESS
Fungible address is always the same, maintained at the protocol level


```solidity

```

### SYSTEM_CONTRACT_ADDRESS


*Name is in upper case to maintain compatibility with ZRC20.sol v1*
